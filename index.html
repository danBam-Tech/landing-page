Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about WAIS.

How does WAIS help reduce bias, ego, and hierarchy within teams?

WAIS guides participants through structured phases that encourage perspective-shifting and balanced participation. All responses are anonymous, allowing ideas to be expressed freely without pressure from hierarchy or dominant voices.

Who is WAIS for?

WAIS is for teams who collaborate to turn ideas into meaningful products. It can be used by students, designers, developers, and teams working in collaborative environments.

Can WAIS replace our usual discussion or brainstorming sessions?

Not at all. WAIS is not meant to replace actual brainstorming sessions, it works best alongside your existing sessions. WAIS complements your discussions by adding structure when teams need clarity, alignment, or guidance.

Is WAIS suitable for non-technical teams?

Yes. WAIS can be used by any team‚Äîcreative, business, academic, or technical‚Äîwho needs a better way to explore and develop ideas. It can also be used for group or family discussions.
