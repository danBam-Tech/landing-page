However, the Application allows you to input information voluntarily, which may include:

The information you input is used solely for the following purposes:

We do not sell, rent, or share any user-provided content—including names or ideas—with third parties.

Your data is processed only within the Application to support functionality.

Your inputs are retained only for as long as required for the Application to function. You may delete your inputs at any time from within the Application (if supported) or by contacting us.

Reasonable security measures are implemented to protect your data. However, no electronic storage method is completely secure, and absolute protection cannot be guaranteed.


Children’s Privacy

WAIS is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect identifiable information from children.


Changes to This Privacy Policy
