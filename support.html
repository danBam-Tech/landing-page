Contact Us

For support inquiries, questions, or feedback, please contact us at:
Email: app@waismind.com
The team aims to respond to all inquiries within 48 hours during business days.

How We Can Help

Our support team can assist you with:

  • Account-related questions and issues
  • Technical problems or bugs
  • Privacy and data concerns
  • Feature requests and feedback
  • Reporting inappropriate content or behavior
  • General questions about using WAIS

Before Contacting Us

To help us assist you more effectively, please include the following information in your message:

  • A clear description of your question or issue
  • Your device type and operating system version
  • App version (if applicable)
  • Steps you've already taken to find the issue
  • Screenshots (if relevant to your issue)

Reporting Issues

If you encounter any bugs, technical issues, or inappropriate content, please report it to us immediately. Your feedback helps us improve WAIS.

Privacy and Data Requests

For questions about your privacy rights, or data deletion requests, please contact us at the email above. You can also review our Privacy Policy for more information about how we handle your data.
